Rikka Takanashi

Rikka Takanashi is the female protagonist in the Japanese light novel series and anime, Chūnibyō Demo Koi ga Shitai!. She is the founder and leader of the club she made named Far Eastern Magic Napping Society of Summer. She is voiced by Maaya Uchida in the anime. In the English Dub, she is voiced by Margaret McDonald.

Rikka Takanashi has blue and yellow eyes.
